IS 601 - History of Internet


The History of the Internet is a website created by two graduate students, Dalitza Sanchez and Luis Chavez Saenz, from NJIT as a required assignment for the IS 601:Web System Development course of the Spring 2021 semester. The content of the website summarizes The Invention of the Internet and The True Story of the Internet documentaries in details.

Within these documentaries there are five main topics:

  • The Invention of the Internet
  • Browser Wars
  • Internet Search
  • Dot Com Bubble
  • People
